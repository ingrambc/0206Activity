Parsons Chameleon

The parsons Chameleon is the second larges chameleon behind the oustalet's. Thaey are the longest living chameleon living up to 15 years. They also are on of the few species that live in the higher forests and require a colder temperature. Like all chameleons they do not drink standing water, and get their water from rain.
